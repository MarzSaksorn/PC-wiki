Building Your First PC

First step into PC Wiki is to Get a computer

If you are using other's computer then... why are you here

Things to get your hand on for you to get started

 
- CPU or Central Processing Unit
(if you do not get a GPU, Ensure that your CPU has an IGPU)
 
- GPU or Graphics Processing Unit
(optional if for office use or do not gaming)
 
- Motherboard
(Find the one that has the same Socket as your CPU and same Ram type as your Ram)
 
- Ram or Random Access Memory
(Match the type of the Ram to the Motherboard)
 
- Storage
(M.2 SSD for Operating system and Apps) / (HDD for long term / Importent data)
 
- CPU Cooler
(Air cooling or Water cooling) Depends on the system
 
- Thermal Paste
(eg. MX-4, MX-5)
 
- PSU or Power Supply Unit
(Choose one that has enough wattage for the whole system)
 
- A Case
(That can fit all the components)
 
- Bunch of Case Fans
(Do not cheap out on this / Get one that is adjustable in color and speed)
